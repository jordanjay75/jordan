Designing a CUI for the blind

Conversational Interfaces are mainstays of homes and mobile decives â€” taking to form of SIRI, Alexa, Cortana, and the Amazon Echo. They can even be found in vehicles as voice assistance that save drives time and provide safety.

For this assignment, my team and I were tasked with developing a domain for a CUI that would assist in novel ways, communicate clear pre-attentive and attentive states, as well as serve as an absolute necessity for a target population.

With this in mind, we set out to build a CUI for the blind that would assist visually impaired shoppers in a dressing room in case of need. This virtual assistant had feautres ranging from: personal style indexing and color matching, to automoated payment methods so the VI wouldnt have to spend too much time searching.
