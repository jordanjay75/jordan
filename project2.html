Stealth IP

Commercialization and Strategy

Working within a 4-person team to identify viable go-to-market opportunities for a Carnegie Mellon Incubated IP

This project is meant to highlight the targeting to internal and external project stakeholders as we explore how the best pricing models, marketing models, and forcast "Startup" growth for venture funding.

Our team is currently focused on positioning the IP for buyout from the univeristy, as well as securing patent rights. The next iterations of this project will involve pitching our price strategy and plans for monetization to a group of VC's for funding.
