Roblox

Product Manager: Text Localization

Roblox helps power the imaginations of people around the world. Every month, over 64 million active players come to Roblox to imagine, create, and play together in immersive 3D worlds, making it the largest social platform for play. We call it the “Imagination Platform” and invite everyone to play on it.

As a PM on the console platform team, I was responsible for mmanaging console product pipelines to support technical and business efforts across data, engineering, web/mobile development, and UX strategy teams for in-game chat features, multiplayer, market expansion, and cross-platform play capabilities.

One of the main projects I worked on was a text localization project that included 20+ langauge translations that would open Roblox to a several new markets in Europe and Asia.
